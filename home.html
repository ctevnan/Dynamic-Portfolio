Creative Expression

Throughout my life I have been a creative individual. I love the creative process. I find great pleasure in visualizing a project, then taking all the steps necessary to make my vision real. For most of my life I accomplished this through attending art classes and working with pencils, clay or pastels as my medium. I have found the perfect medium in web design. A quote from Mark Boulton sums up my approach to design; "I think design covers so much more than the aesthetic. Design is fundamentally more. Design is usability. Its Information Architecture. Its accessibility. This is design."

Passion for Sustainability

The design aspects of being a front end web developer do intrigue me, but what is design without meaningful content? This bring me to my second reason for choosing web development as a career, which is the simple fact that I want to fulfill my passion for working in the field of Environmental Sustainability. While in college, I fulfilled this passion by creating and leading a subcommittee focused cutting down food waste on campus and establishing a composting program. Now, I have skills that can take my passion for sustainability to the next level.
