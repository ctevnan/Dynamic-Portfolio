Welcome to Carolyn Tevnan's Portfolio Page

Lets start with the three main reasons why I want to be a web developer;

Creative Expression

Throughout my life I have been a creative individual. I love the creative process. I find great pleasure in visualizing a project, then taking all the steps necessary to make my vision real. For most of my life I accomplished this through being in advanced art classes and working with pencils, clay or pastels as my medium. I have found the perfect medium in web design. A quote from Mark Boulton sums up my approach to design; "I think design covers so much more than the aesthetic. Design is fundamentally more. Design is usability. Its Information Architecture. Its accessibility. This is design."

Reaching Earnest Goals

The design aspects of being a front end web developer do intrigue me, but what is design without meaningful content? This bring me to my second reason for choosing web development as a career, which is the simple fact that I want to fulfill my passion for working in the field of Environmental Sustainability. While in college, I fulfilled this passion by creating and leading a subcommittee focused cutting down food waste on campus and establishing a composting program. Now, I have skills that can take my passion for sustainability to the next level.

Culture Fit

I understand how important it is to be a good fit culturally speaking when it comes to joining a new company. Although I do not have a formal tech background, I do have lots of friends who work in the field, specifically in cloud computing. Who initially inspired me to start gaining technical skills? My friends who work in the field! I loved the conversations that they would have, and I wanted to join the discusssion. I began by studying computer hardware, and to this day I enjoy doing hardware installations in my free time. It just so happens that my true love is software.
